# Smelting

Build smelting as continuous raw-resource flow rather than furnace islands.

- Trace ore from extraction through transport into each furnace.
- Trace fuel from its source through transport into every burner consumer.
- Provide automated output removal before adding more furnaces.
- Route plates toward their actual consumers or an explicit stockpile
  milestone; do not rely on character collection between stages.
- Expand only after one complete lane passes sustained verification.
