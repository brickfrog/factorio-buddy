# Power

Treat electricity as a production chain with water, fuel, generation,
distribution, and demand dependencies.

- Inspect existing generation and networks before adding another plant.
- Ensure fuel reaches generation automatically; stored fuel alone is a finite
  buffer, not sustainable supply.
- Verify fluid continuity and generation status before extending poles.
- Connect consumers to the intended network and confirm demand is served.
- Prove the network remains available during the observation window without
  character fueling or repair.
